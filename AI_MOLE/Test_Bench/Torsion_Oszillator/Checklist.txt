%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Neuen AI-MOLE Run beginnen
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1.  AI-MOLE objekt mit 'Init_MOLE' mit gewünschten Parametern erzeugen
2.  AI-MOLE objekt in Initialisierungsskript des Prüfstandes auswählen
    ('ini_TSchw_SS25_MOLE')
3.  AI-MOLE objekt in Updateskript auswählen ('MOLE_torsion_oszillator_step')
4.  Konfiguration des Simulink Modells überprüfen (alle switches richtig gesetzt)

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
AI-MOLE Step durchführen
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1.  Initialisierungsskript ('ini_TSchw_SS25_MOLE') ausführen (Eingangstrajektorie
    wird in Workspace geladen - zugriff von Simulink Modell aus)
2.  Simulink laufen lassen (zuvor Konfiguration prüfen)
3.  Skript 'Load_trial_results' ausführen, um die für AI-MOLE passende 
    Ausgangstrajektorie (angepasste Abtastzeit Ts) zu erzeugen
4.  Skript 'MOLE_torsion_oszillator_step' ausführen, um AI-MOLE update 
    durchzuführen (Aktuatortrajektorie überprüfen)

----> zurück zu Schritt 1, bis alle Iterationen durchgeführt


%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Abtastzeit ändern
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1.  Referenztrajektorie mit gewünschter Abtastzeit erstellen und AI-MOLE 
    Objekt basierend auf dieser erstellen
2.  Referenztrajektorie in 'ini_TSchw_SS25_MOLE' ändern
3.  ILC Abtastzeit in 'Load_trial_results' anpassen
