%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Neuen ILC Run beginnen
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1.  ILC objekt mit 'Init_ILC' mit gewünschten Parametern erzeugen
2.  ILC objekt in Initialisierungsskript des Prüfstandes auswählen
    ('ini_TSchw_SS25_ILC')
3.  AI-MOLE objekt in Updateskript auswählen ('ILC_torsion_oszillator_step')
4.  Konfiguration des Simulink Modells überprüfen (alle switches richtig gesetzt)

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
AI-MOLE Step durchführen
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1.  Initialisierungsskript ('ini_TSchw_SS25_ILC') ausführen (Eingangstrajektorie
    wird in Workspace geladen - zugriff von Simulink Modell aus)
2.  Simulink laufen lassen (zuvor Konfiguration prüfen)
3.  Skript 'Load_trial_results' ausführen, um die für ILC passende 
    Ausgangstrajektorie (angepasste Abtastzeit Ts) zu erzeugen
4.  Skript 'ILC_torsion_oszillator_step' ausführen, um ILC update 
    durchzuführen (Aktuatortrajektorie überprüfen)
